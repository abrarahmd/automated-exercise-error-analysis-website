Representation Engineering

A Top-Down Approach to AI Transparency

Understanding and Controlling the Inner Workings of Neural Networks

We identify and characterize the emerging area of representation engineering (RepE), an approach to enhancing the transparency of AI systems that draws on insights from cognitive neuroscience.

What is RepE?

RepE places representations, rather than neurons or circuits, at the center of analysis, equipping us with novel methods for monitoring and manipulating high-level cognitive phenomena in deep neural networks.

Transparency

We provide baselines and an initial analysis of RepE techniques, showing that they offer simple yet effective solutions for improving our understanding and control of large language models.

Applications

This approach enables AI "mind reading and control" by performing top-down transparency analysis inspired by cognitive neuroscience methods.
